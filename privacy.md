Privacy Policy for TakeWebNotes
Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction
   TakeWebNotes ("the Extension") is a productivity tool designed to help users take context-aware notes tied to specific websites. We believe your data is yours, and we are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Data Collection and Usage
   TakeWebNotes operates entirely on your local device.

Web History (Tabs): The Extension requires the tabs permission to read the URL (hostname) of your currently active tab. This is strictly used locally to display the correct notes for the website you are visiting. This data is never tracked, logged, or sent to any external server.

Storage: Your notes and task lists are saved locally on your device and synced across your devices using Google Chrome's native chrome.storage.sync API. We do not have access to this data.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure
   We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any of your personal data.

We do not sell or transfer user data to third parties.

We do not use or transfer user data for purposes unrelated to the Extension's core functionality.

We do not use or transfer user data to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.

4. Third-Party Services
   TakeWebNotes does not integrate with any external APIs, analytics trackers, or third-party servers.

5. Changes to This Policy
   We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in the Extension's features or Chrome Web Store policies.

6. Contact
   If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store support tab.
